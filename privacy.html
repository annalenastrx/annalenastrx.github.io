OVERVIEW

The AR IoT privacy app was developed to provide insights into data collection of IoT devices. Our app recognizes unique markers to detect IoT devices and visualize their sensors in augmented reality. Depending on sensors and technologies built in the device, the sensors are displayed through icons. Additionally, we provide a data protection rating and a creepyness score visualized through an emoji based on Mozilla's privacy not included guide. In the same way as in the guide, we also implemented a creepyness voting slider.

We implemented three different designs and conducted a user study to compare them and receive feedback. In the future it would be possible to replace markers by device recognition.

MY ROLE

Group project with two fellow students. I was responsible for including the AR Foundations Framework, implementing the marker recognition and the interactions. I also contributed to voting data export. Together we developed the concept for the app and conducted a user study.
